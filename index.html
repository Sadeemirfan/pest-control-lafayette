Because of Louisiana’s warm, humid climate, pests are active year-round — unlike in colder parts of the country where pest activity slows significantly in winter. For most homes in the Lafayette area, we recommend quarterly general pest control service (every three months) to maintain effective protection against ants, cockroaches, spiders, and other common household pests. For termite protection, an annual inspection combined with an ongoing baiting or liquid barrier system is standard. Mosquito control treatments are typically applied monthly during the peak season from March through October. We will work with you to design a service schedule that makes sense for your specific pest pressures and property type.

It depends on the type of treatment being performed. For standard general pest control applications such as perimeter sprays, crack-and-crevice treatments, and gel baiting, you typically do not need to vacate your home. For more intensive treatments such as full-home fumigation or heat treatment for bed bugs, you will need to be out of the home for a longer period, which we will discuss with you in advance. A little preparation goes a long way in helping us achieve the best results. Before our technician arrives, we recommend clearing clutter from under sinks, behind appliances, and inside closets to allow access to common pest harborage areas. Store all food items in sealed containers and wipe down kitchen counters and stovetops. If you have pets, place food and water bowls out of the way and cover any fish tanks. For rodent treatments, remove any items stored directly on the floor around the perimeter of your garage or attic. For termite and mosquito treatments, we will provide you with specific preparation instructions when you book your appointment.
